What is Decentral Bank?


Decentral is a Colored Coins wallet that let's users issue tokens representing property, coins or
businesses. Decentral's goal is to make crowdfunding easier and more powerful for everyone.

What's the problem?



Bitcoin does not replace banks, our current financial system still needs them.

Bitcoin still lacks an investment and reputation infrastructure.

Bitcoin users still have to rely on a third party to handle their deposits honestly.

Features

Tokenization

Decentral aims to replace some of the main functions of banks such as the ability to issue, buy. and sell shares/tokens of any entity whose tokens are issued through Bitcoin 2.0 technologies such as colored coins.



Multisignature

Secure Wallet. Easy-to-use multisignature bitcoin wallet. With Decentral Wallet you can choose to have more than one person or device sign a transaction. Should your private keys be compromised your Bitcoins are still safe!

Voting Pools

Bitcoin users that have to rely on a third party to handle their funds have been victims of heists, thefts, hacks, and scams. We will build on top of Open Transactions technology to solve this problem.
