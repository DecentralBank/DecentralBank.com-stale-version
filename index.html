Build on Top of Bitcoin

Coins,Tokens,Property

Decentral is a Colored Coins Wallet that lets users issue tokens representing property, coins or
businesses
Decentral's goal is to make crowdfunding easier and more powerful for everyone

What's the problem?

Bitcoin does not replace banks, our current financial system still needs them.

Bitcoin still lacks an investment and reputation infrastructure

Bitcoin users still have to rely on a third party to handle their deposits honestly

Tokenization

Decentral aims to replace some of the main functions of banks such as the ability to issue, buy and sell shares/tokens of any entity whose tokens are issued through Bitcoin2.0 technologies such as colored coins

Multisignature

Secure Wallet. Easy-to-use multisignature bitcoin wallet. With Decentral Wallet you can choose to have more than one person or device sign a transaction. Should your private keys be compromised your Bitcoins are still safe

Voting pools

Bitcoin users that have to rely on a third party to handle their funds has been a victim of heists, thefts, hacks, scams, and losses. We will build on top of Open Transactions technology to solve this problem

Features

Multisignatures, Offline mode, Colored Bitcoins, Decentralized exchange, Open Source.
